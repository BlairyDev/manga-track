Blairnicorn

I still remember the first time I read the forest training arc, and how excited I got with every chapter. It's a really nice memory. ...It has been a long time since the series has been able to capture that level of hype for me. At this point I'm just reading new chapters on autopilot. It's a damn shame because the author was able to maintain a pretty decent story quality for a long time before dropping off. When you start hoping for a manga to end quickly with every chapter, you know it's not going to come back to that level any more. I just hope the ending isn't too bad.
